**Playground** exercises one **model route** without going through a real business **user API key**—ideal after editing providers/routes.

<Aside type="tip" title="When to use">
	After route changes or vendor migrations, validate before shifting production traffic.
</Aside>

## How to use

<Steps>
1. Open **Playground** in Admin.
1. Pick a **model route** (active routes should appear).
1. Fill a payload matching the protocol (e.g. OpenAI `messages`).
1. **Send** and inspect status, upstream errors, latency.
1. On failure, revisit [Providers](/en/docs/guides/providers/) and [Routing](/en/docs/guides/routing/), then [Request logs](/en/docs/guides/request-logs/).
</Steps>

## vs real traffic

Playground is operator-focused; metering may differ from real user-key calls (per release). For full client simulation, use [Simulator](/en/docs/features/simulator/).
