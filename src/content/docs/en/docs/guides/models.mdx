**Models** are the gateway catalog: client **`model`** strings align with **model ids** here, then [Routing](/en/docs/guides/routing/) picks upstreams.

<Aside type="tip" title="Prerequisite">
	Have at least one [Provider](/en/docs/guides/providers/).
</Aside>

## In Admin

<Steps>
1. Open **Models**.
1. **Create/import** entries: **model id**, display name, protocol flavor (per UI).
1. **Suppliers vs routes**: models do not have to pin a single vendor; routing decides the upstream.
1. **After saving**: create at least one **active** route in [Routing](/en/docs/guides/routing/).
</Steps>

## Route groups

Clients may send **`baseId:group`** (e.g. `my-model:free`). Canonical parsing rules: [GitHub technical reference](/en/docs/github-reference/) → **User API** doc.
