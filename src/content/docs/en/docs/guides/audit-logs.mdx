Open **User → Audit Logs**. Entries here focus on **what changed for accounts and budgets**: budget updates, spend deductions, user/key lifecycle events, and Admin edits to users or keys, often correlated to a key or request context.

This is **not** a full audit trail of every **provider/route/settings** click in Admin. For configuration change governance, use your change-management process or a separate ops audit trail.

<Aside type="tip" title="Versus request logs">
	**Request logs**: per-inference HTTP traces. **Audit logs**: budget and identity/key lifecycle events.
</Aside>

## What to do in Admin

<Steps>
1. Open **Audit Logs**.
1. Filter by time window, user email, key, event type, actor type, reason code, source, and other fields shown in the UI.
1. Expand rows for budget notes, user snapshot deltas, and change details (fields duplicated in the table may be hidden).
1. For a single user, you can also open [Users](/en/docs/guides/users/) → user detail for related snippets.
</Steps>
