**Audit logs** focus on **who changed what** in Admin—distinct from **request logs** (inference traffic).

<Aside type="tip" title="Prerequisite">
	Appropriate read-only or auditor access.
</Aside>

## In Admin

<Steps>
1. Open **Audit logs**.
1. **Filter** by actor, object type, time window.
1. **Drill into users** when links are available.
1. **Export / SIEM** per organizational policy.
</Steps>
