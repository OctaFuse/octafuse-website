## Problems we focus on

When many products or teams need LLMs, friction tends to pile up:

- **Repeated work**: each service wires providers, keys, and limits on its own—hard to standardize and expensive to maintain.
- **Fragmented ecosystems**: different clients and vendors mean more adapters, more surprises, and more operational load.
- **Cost and risk**: without a single view of “who used what, how much, and why,” budgeting, investigations, and governance get painful.
- **Reliability pressure**: upstream changes, model swaps, and safe rollouts are much harder without a central place to govern routing.

**OctaFuse** is an **AI gateway platform** for teams and organizations: it centralizes model access, routing policy, budgets and usage, and operator tooling so product teams can stay focused on what they ship—not on re-implementing gateway plumbing everywhere.

## Deployment choices

Pick what fits compliance, latency, and ops: **managed cloud** (fast to ship, edge-friendly) or **self-hosted** (stricter data residency and network boundaries). See [Deployment](/en/docs/deploy/); licensing is **AGPLv3** — full text in [LICENSE](https://github.com/OctaFuse/octafuse-gateway/blob/main/LICENSE).

## Who it’s for

- Platform and product orgs that need **one place to govern model traffic** across many lines of business or tenants.
- Teams that want **cost, reliability, and auditability** lifted out of application code into dedicated infrastructure.
- Organizations that want **freedom to evolve vendors and models** without every service carrying bespoke failover logic.
